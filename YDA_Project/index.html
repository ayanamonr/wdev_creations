YDA

Mission

LRHS Young Democrats is an inclusive political club that encourages students to become aware of current events, advocate for causes in their local communities, and discuss progressive issues, and assist their peers in becoming active and informed citizens.

Throughout the Year Meetings will Include:

  1. Monthly Topics of Discussions
  2. Guest Speakers
  3. Advocating for Representatives
  4. Supporting Local, State, and Federal Causes
  5. Much More!
